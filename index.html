Our Mission

The Problem

Electronic waste is the fastest-growing waste stream in the world, with over 50 million metric tons generated annually. This e-waste contains hazardous materials that can leach into soil and water, posing serious environmental and health risks.

Our Solution

IWB PTY LTD provides comprehensive e-waste recycling solutions that ensure electronic devices are disposed of responsibly. We recover valuable materials, safely handle hazardous components, and help businesses meet their sustainability goals.
